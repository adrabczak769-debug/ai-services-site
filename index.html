Common questions

Does the AI really sound human? add
Yes. The voice agents we deploy use the same technology behind the most advanced AI phone systems. They handle natural conversation, pauses, interruptions, and even accent-matching. Most callers don't realize they're talking to AI until told.
How long does setup take? add
Most clients are live in 3-5 business days. We handle everything: writing the script, training the AI on your business, connecting it to your calendar and CRM. You just review and approve before it goes live.
What if the AI can't answer a question? add
It gracefully transfers the call to you or a staff member. You set the threshold — for complex jobs or specific situations, the AI hands off smoothly without the caller feeling dropped. It also logs everything so you can follow up.
Do I have to change my phone number? add
No. We use call forwarding so your existing number stays the same. Calls just get routed through the AI before reaching you. Customers always see your real business number on caller ID.
Is there a contract or commitment? add
Month-to-month. No annual contracts, no cancellation fees. We keep you because the system works, not because you're locked in. That said, most clients stick around because they'd never go back to missing calls.
How do I know it's actually working? add
You get full call recordings, a live dashboard, and a monthly performance report showing calls handled, leads booked, and revenue recovered. Nothing is hidden.
What if I don't like it after a month? add
Cancel with one email. No penalty, no fine print. We keep your business because the system works, not because you're locked in.
Is it actually AI, or is a person answering my calls? add
