FAQ

Answers before you ask.
No fluff.

Will candidates know they're talking to an AI?+
Yes — and it doesn't hurt conversion. Candidates in light industrial and commercial staffing are used to automated text communication. What kills engagement is silence. The AI responds in 90 seconds. Your team might respond in two days.
Do I need to change my ATS or job board setup?+
No. We connect to whatever you're already using — Indeed, ZipRecruiter, your website, a Google Form — via webhook or email forwarding. Nothing in your current workflow changes. Setup takes 3–5 business days.
What if a candidate asks something the AI can't handle?+
The AI knows its limits. If a candidate asks something outside its scope, it flags the conversation and routes it to your team with a full transcript. Your recruiter picks up mid-conversation — not starting from zero.
How long does setup take?+
Most agencies are live in 5 business days. Day 1–2: we build your screening flow. Day 3: we connect your intake sources. Day 4: testing. Day 5: you go live. We handle everything technical — you just approve the questions.
Is there a long-term contract?+
Month-to-month. No annual commitment, no cancellation fee. We keep clients by getting results, not by locking them in. If the system isn't working after 60 days, you cancel and owe nothing extra.
What if I want to change the screening questions later?+
Takes 24 hours. Email or text us the change, we update the flow and send you a test conversation to approve. Growth plan clients get same-day turnaround and unlimited updates.
